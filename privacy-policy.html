Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

FamilyHub School ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • Full name: Displayed in the app and used for task assignments
  • Profile photo: Optional, if you choose to upload one

User Content

We store the content you create in the app:

  • Homework assignments, tests, and activities
  • Task details, due dates, and completion status
  • Grade records and test scores
  • Family member information you add
  • Notes and descriptions you create

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about how you use the app:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App interactions and feature usage
  • Error logs and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and sync your tasks, grades, and family data
  • Send local notifications for task reminders (with your permission)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted backend service provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Family Members: Information you add to shared family accounts is visible to other family members you invite
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services to operate our app:
    • Supabase (database and authentication)
    • RevenueCat (subscription management)
    • Apple (In-App Purchases)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety

5. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store and managed via RevenueCat. We do not store your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy.

6. Notifications

With your permission, we schedule local notifications on your device to remind you of upcoming task due dates. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve sending data to external servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for family use, including children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. Parents and guardians are responsible for managing their family accounts and the information added about children.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal information stored in the app
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through the app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications in your device settings
  • Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us

To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently delete your personal information and user content within 30 days. Some information may be retained longer if required by law.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed in any country where we or our service providers maintain facilities. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Third-Party Links

Our app does not contain links to third-party websites or services. If we add such links in the future, we are not responsible for their privacy practices.
